Sign Language - A beautiful expressive language

People with hearing loss and speech impairement cannot use acoustic languages for their communication. Sign Language is a language that conveys the message by using body language instead of the acoustic words. There are around 466 million people worldwide with hearing loss and 34 million of these are children. World Health Organization (WHO) ‘‘factsheet’’ states that teaching sign language will benefit children with hearing loss.
